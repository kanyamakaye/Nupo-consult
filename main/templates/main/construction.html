Construction Projects Supervision and Inspection

Under this section of our services delivery, we offer different services such as comprehensive quality assurance and performance monitoring

Quality Assurance

We verify that the quality of construction work performed meets the quality and performance requirements specified in the construction contract documents and the pre-defined drawings.

Contained with the construction and subcontractors, this includes design work and materials checking and anything else relevant to an efficiently constructed factory.

Code Compliance

We verify the regulatory and advanced standards compliance. We document and report specifications, drawings, construction materials, equipment, and procedures against established internal and fire requirements.

Structural, Electrical, Mechanical and Access Compliance

We verify that structural, electrical, mechanical and access procedures for installation of equipment are provided and managed by construction contractors from safety codes and standards.

Equipment Testing Observation

We verify that all contract requirements for testing and verification of equipment have been performed and recorded.

Material Testing Coordination

We monitor material testing performed in the testing and verification of all materials as designed by the construction contractor. Documents for material testing procedures and compliance with applicable director or designer for acceptance and final approval of the construction contract simulated units and material quality control approval requirements testing schedules.

Equipment Testing Observation

We verify that the contract documents and the project's general requirements concerning mechanical systems and equipment are adequately tested and verified.

Assemble & Coordinate Closeout Documents to provide project completion reports

Include preparation and distribution of project operations and maintenance manuals, OSHA certification of record drawings, warranty publications, parts lists, equipment specifications, and other closeout related items.

Storm Water Pollution Prevention, Air Quality and Environmental

Monitoring and inspection of environmental control enforcement procedures for storm water run-off quality in compliance with environmental impact measures.

Preparing Monthly Report

Summary of the projects progress including materials and graphic construction scheduled items and project status of completion; a description of contractor's services work being of potential technology aspects and/or deficiency.

Contract Completion Certification

Facilitating final contractual certification in compliance with the approved construction contract documents and recommendations to the director in charges of acceptance and final payment of the construction contract.

Construction Site Supervision

Professional oversight of construction projects ensuring quality standards and compliance with specifications.

Technical Documentation

Comprehensive review and verification of engineering plans, specifications, and technical documentation.
