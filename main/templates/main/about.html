Who We Are

An engineering consulting firm registered and domiciled in Rwanda, with principal consultancy activities of engineering Projects Management, Construction Management, Design Management, construction permit procedure, Tender Management, Client Representation and other engineering Consultancy services such as Value Engineering / Design Review, Cost Analysis and Estimation, Procurement, legal services, Claims Management and troubleshooting.

NUPO Consult Ltd promotes and at the same time strategic leaders are highly qualified and experienced professionals who endowed with high level of understanding of all aspects of engineering and ready to assist in shaping the direction of its current and prospective clients.

We have expertise in different engineering intervention such as Architectural Engineering, Structural Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering, Construction Management, Electrical Engineering, Traffic and Transportation Engineering, Hydraulics and Water Resources Engineering and Geo-informatics.
