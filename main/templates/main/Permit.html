Construction Permit Procedure

Generally, you need to obtain a building/construction permit from the City whenever you construct, enlarge, alter, repair, move, remove, improve, convert, or demolish a building or other structure. We assist and help our clients in the application of construction permit by submitting complete and legible plans that clearly indicate and describe new development and any modification to existing buildings, or any other structure.

New Construction

Building new structures from ground up

Renovation

Alterations and improvements

Demolition

Safe removal of structures

Extensions

Enlarging existing buildings

We help our clients to prepare all the required documents for the authorities to issue construction/building permit as per relevant authority guidelines and regulations.
